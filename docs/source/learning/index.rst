.. _learning:

Learning
========

To make the ``QSDsan`` platform more accessible, we host workshops and develop
educational materials that walk through the steps of Quantitative Sustainable Design
(QSD) and how they are executed using ``QSDsan``.

Workshops
---------

.. grid:: 1 1 2 2
   :gutter: 4

   .. grid-item-card:: QSD Workshop at the AEESP Research and Education Conference
      :img-top: ../images/learning/aeesp.png

      During the 2022 `Association of Environmental Engineering and Science Professors
      (AEESP) Research and Education Conference <https://aeesp2022.wustl.edu>`_, we hosted
      a workshop on Quantitative Sustainable Design (QSD), familiarizing participants with
      a structured approach to sustainable design and providing experience in leveraging
      QSD to generate insight in their domains of interest.

   .. grid-item-card:: QSDsan Workshop at the EES Symposium
      :img-top: ../images/learning/ees.png

      We hosted an entry-level ``QSDsan`` workshop at the 27th `Environmental Engineering
      and Science (EES) Symposium <https://publish.illinois.edu/2022-environmentalsymposium/>`_
      at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, where attendees interacted with
      ``QSDsan`` through `Binder <https://mybinder.org/>`_ with no local installation or
      prior programming knowledge required.
